### Commercial Mathematics Formulae

#### Simple Interest

$$ 
    \text{Simple Interest = } \frac {N \times P \times R} {100} 
$$ 
where, \
$ \text{N} $ = Time period, \
$ \text{P} $ = Principal amount and \
$ \text{R} $ = Rate of Interest
