### Geometry & Mensuration Formulae

#### Rectangle

1. Area of rectangle

    $$
     \text{Area = } l \times b 
    $$ 

    where, \
    $ \text{l} $ = length and \
    $ \text{b} $ = breadth

#### Square

1. Area of square

    $$
     \text{Area = } side \times side = (side)^2
    $$ 

    where, \
    $ \text{l} $ = length and \
    $ \text{b} $ = breadth

#### Circle

1. Area of circle

    $$ 
        \text{Area = } \pi \times r^2 
    $$

    where, \
    $ \text{r} $ = radius

#### Cylinder

1. Volume of cylinder

    $$ 
        \text{Volume = } \pi \times r^2 \times h
    $$

    where, \
    $ \text{r} $ = radius of base and \
    $ \text{h} $ = height of cylinder
